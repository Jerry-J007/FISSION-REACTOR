# FISSION-REACTOR
☢️ FISSION REACTOR
Hey! Welcome to FISSION REACTOR.
GAMEPLAY LINK - https://youtu.be/3nIikjW1obo

A fast-paced, grid-based tactical game where up to 4 players fight to trigger massive chain reactions and wipe each other off the map. 

If you like outsmarting your friends, stealing their hard-earned points, and setting off chain reactions that wipe out half the board in a single turn, you're in the right place.

🎮 How to Play
The game happens on a 12x6 grid. You take turns dropping "Atoms" (neon energy orbs) into cells.

The Catch: Every cell has a limit. (Corners can only hold 1 atom, edges hold 2, and the center cells hold 3).

The Boom: If you drop an atom into a cell that is already full, it goes critical. It explodes, shooting your atoms into the neighboring cells and instantly stealing any enemy atoms that were sitting there!

The Goal: Wipe everyone else out, or have the most points when the 3-minute master clock runs out.

🚀 The Fun Stuff (Mechanics)
I wanted to make sure no corner of the map ever felt truly "safe," so I added a few crazy mechanics to shake things up:

🌀 Spatial Relay Portals
There are two highly unstable "Teleport Pads" permanently locked onto the board. If your chain reaction hits one of these portals, the atom doesn't stop—it instantly teleports out of the other portal on the opposite side of the map! It is the perfect way to launch a surprise attack on someone who thinks they are hiding safely across the grid.

⚡ Power-Ups (Bombs & Hacking)
If you manage to set off a massive combo that scores a ton of points in a single turn, the system rewards you with tactical abilities (you can hoard up to 5 of each!):

Deploy Bomb (150+ point combo): Lets you call in a tactical strike anywhere on the board, instantly vaporizing a 3x3 area. It doesn't trigger explosions, making it perfect for deleting heavily defended enemy bases.

System Override (300+ point combo): This is just straight-up theft. You instantly hack into an enemy cell and flip all of their atoms to your color.

⏱️ Speed Chess Timers
I added strict timers because this game is way more fun when people are panicking:

15-Second Turns: You have exactly 15 seconds to make a move. If you take too long, the system skips you!

3-Minute Meltdown: The game has a hard stop at 3 minutes. If it's a standoff, the reactor freezes and the system crowns whoever caused the most destruction as the winner.
